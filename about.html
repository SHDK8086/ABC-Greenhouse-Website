About ABC Greenhouse Solutions

Pioneering sustainable agriculture in Sri Lanka for over a decade, transforming the way we grow and consume fresh produce.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Sri Lanka's leading greenhouse solutions provider

Who We Are

ABC Greenhouse Solutions was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize agriculture in Sri Lanka through innovative greenhouse technology. Based in the beautiful city of Kandy, we've grown from a small startup to a leading force in sustainable farming.

Our team of agricultural experts, engineers, and sustainability advocates work tirelessly to provide cutting-edge solutions that not only increase productivity but also protect our environment for future generations.

What We Do

We specialize in controlled environment agriculture, utilizing state-of-the-art greenhouse technology to produce the highest quality organic fruits and vegetables year-round. Our climate-controlled environments ensure optimal growing conditions regardless of external weather patterns.

Beyond production, we also design and construct custom greenhouse solutions for farmers and businesses looking to embrace sustainable agriculture. Our comprehensive services include maintenance, technical support, and agricultural consulting.
